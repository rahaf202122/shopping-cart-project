☕ About Almond Cake ☕

Welcome to Almond Cake ☕ — a modern café and dessert shop created by Rahaf Salahat as a creative web development project.

Almond Cake combines delicious coffee drinks, refreshing mojitos, sweet desserts, cookies, cakes, ice cream, and premium shisha flavors in one relaxing and stylish place.

This project reflects my passion for programming, creativity, and modern café design. I wanted to create a warm digital experience using soft turquoise, white, and beige colors to give customers a cozy and elegant feeling.

Every section of this website was customized and developed as part of my JavaScript learning journey while improving my frontend development skills.

Thank you for visiting Almond Cake ✨
